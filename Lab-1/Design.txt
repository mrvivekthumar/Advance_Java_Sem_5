Design :
1. Interface: Engine
    o Method: startEngine(), stopEngine()
2. Abstract Class: Vehicle
    o Common properties: model
    o Common method: displayInfo()
3. Concrete Classes:
    o Car: Extends Vehicle
    o Truck: Extends Vehicle
    o Bicycle: Extends Vehicle
4. Object Composition:
    o A Car and Truck have an Engine object as a part of their composition
