Design :
1. Interface: EmployeePredicate
    o Method: test(Employee employee) (used for filtering)
    o Default methods:  byAge(int age), 
                        byDepartment(String department)
                        (for common filtering operations)
2. Class: Employee
    o Properties: name, age, salary, department
    o Methods: getters and setters
3. Main Class: EmployeeMain
    o Create a list of employees
    o Use lambda expressions to filter and process the list
    o Calculate statistics like average salary
